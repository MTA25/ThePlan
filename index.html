AI Learning Pipeline

The system learns general design patterns from many contributors. It never copies a single player's room directly.

Robot Interior Designer improves over time

The AI robot can generate room layouts, recommend furniture, optimize lighting, suggest color palettes, create floor plans, and adapt to user preferences.

Investor protection

How Capital Is Controlled

The stated ranges are funding capacity—not permission to spend. Capital is released against evidence, with board visibility into burn, runway, delivery, and commercial performance.

01

Tranche funding

Approve 6–12 months of runway at a time. The next tranche requires delivery, financial, and risk gates.

02

Monthly reporting

Budget versus actual, headcount, committed spend, cash balance, runway, forecast-at-completion, and variance explanations.

03

Procurement controls

Competitive bids, approval limits, separation of duties, contract register, software-seat audits, and cloud-spend alerts.

04

Product evidence

Retention, engagement, payer conversion, ARPDAU, acquisition cost, lifetime value, uptime, and content velocity.

05

Commercial evidence

Bookings, recognized revenue, gross margin, pipeline coverage, renewal, marketplace volume, and partner concentration.

06

Independent assurance

Annual audit, security review, data/privacy assessment, IP ownership verification, and model-risk evaluation.

Minimum runway policy18 months
Budget reportingMonthly
Reforecast cycleQuarterly
Funding releaseMilestone based

A credible investment memorandum must also include cap table, funding instrument, use-of-proceeds schedule, downside case, exit assumptions, comparable-company evidence, risk register, audited historic spend, and a five-year integrated P&L, cash-flow, and balance-sheet model.

  • Furniture stores, smart home brands, developers, brokers, hotels, and architecture studios.

Retention Engine

Go To Market

A phased rollout builds credibility before scaling globally.

Step 1

Private prototype demo for investors, game advisors, interior designers, brokers, and Dubai real estate partners.

Step 2

Closed alpha with invited creators and design-focused players to test room-building, contracts, and rewards.

Step 3

Public beta in UAE and GCC markets with competitions, influencer campaigns, and waitlist rewards.

Step 4

Global mobile launch with marketplace, sponsored challenges, seasonal content, and first AI design previews.

Step 5

Commercial AI launch for homeowners, developers, brokers, furniture brands, and professional designers.
