Players Everywhere

The concept is designed for a wide audience: casual players, design lovers, students, professionals, and families who want creative gameplay with real earning potential.

AI Learning Pipeline

The system learns general design patterns from many contributors. It never copies a single player's room directly.

Player creates room designs
Anonymous design data is collected
Furniture combinations are analyzed
Color and lighting preferences are mapped
Layout relationships become training signals
AI model learns popular design patterns
Robot Interior Designer improves over time

The AI robot can generate room layouts, recommend furniture, optimize lighting, suggest color palettes, create floor plans, and adapt to user preferences.

Budget & Investment

A staged investment plan keeps risk controlled while proving gameplay, audience demand, data value, and commercial AI potential.

Prototype Build $75k - $150k Core Dubai scene, player movement, one apartment, furniture placement, UI prototype, and pitch-ready demo.
Mobile Alpha $250k - $500k Contracts, economy, 50+ furniture assets, saving system, basic analytics, and closed testing.
Public Beta $750k - $1.5M Expanded Dubai map, vehicles, NPCs, marketplace, cloud backend, competitions, and creator tools.
AI Platform Launch $2M - $5M AI training pipeline, professional design robot, brand integrations, enterprise partnerships, and global city expansion.

Go To Market

Step 1

Step 2

Step 3

Public beta in UAE and GCC markets with competitions, influencer campaigns, and waitlist rewards.

Step 4

Global mobile launch with marketplace, sponsored challenges, seasonal content, and first AI design previews.

Step 5

Commercial AI launch for homeowners, developers, brokers, furniture brands, and professional designers.
