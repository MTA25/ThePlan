Investor protection

How Capital Is Controlled

The stated ranges are funding capacity—not permission to spend. Capital is released against evidence, with board visibility into burn, runway, delivery, and commercial performance.

01

Tranche funding

Approve 6–12 months of runway at a time. The next tranche requires delivery, financial, and risk gates.

02

Monthly reporting

Budget versus actual, headcount, committed spend, cash balance, runway, forecast-at-completion, and variance explanations.

03

Procurement controls

Competitive bids, approval limits, separation of duties, contract register, software-seat audits, and cloud-spend alerts.

04

Product evidence

Retention, engagement, payer conversion, ARPDAU, acquisition cost, lifetime value, uptime, and content velocity.

05

Commercial evidence

Bookings, recognized revenue, gross margin, pipeline coverage, renewal, marketplace volume, and partner concentration.

06

Independent assurance

Annual audit, security review, data/privacy assessment, IP ownership verification, and model-risk evaluation.
